CHAPTER 7 — PURCHASING & PROCUREMENT

7.1  General Principles
All purchases must be made in the interest of client care or
operational necessity. Employees are expected to exercise
prudent financial judgment at all times.

7.2  Emergency Ward Supplies
Small consumables (gloves, tape, disinfectant, etc.) may be
purchased without prior approval up to EUR 50,00 per
transaction. Receipts must be submitted within 48 hours.

7.3  Resident Birthday Allowances
Each residential group may spend up to EUR 15,00 per resident
birthday for cake, decorations, and small gifts. Receipts are
to be submitted via the group leader.

7.4  Travel Reimbursement
Business travel by private car is reimbursed at EUR 0,23 per
kilometre. Public transport costs are reimbursed at actual
cost with proof of purchase.

7.5  Daily Groceries — Residential Groups
Each residential group has a daily budget of EUR 15,00 for
fresh groceries (bread, dairy, fruit, vegetables). Purchases
must be made at approved supermarkets.

7.6  Temporary Staff
Hiring of temporary staff must be coordinated through HR.
Agency contracts require approval from the location manager.
No per-transaction spending limit applies, but all invoices
must reference a valid purchase order number.
