SECTION 3 — SPENDING LIMITS & APPROVAL THRESHOLDS

3.1  Purpose
These guidelines supersede previously published limits where
conflicts exist. In case of ambiguity, the Finance department
should be consulted.

3.2  Resident Birthday Allowances
Effective 2025, the maximum birthday spend per resident is
increased to EUR 25,00 to account for inflation. This applies
to all residential care locations.

3.3  Travel Reimbursement
The reimbursement rate for private car use is set at
EUR 0,21 per kilometre, aligned with the revised internal
cost model. The tax-free allowance remains EUR 0,23/km.

3.4  Office Supplies
Office supplies must be ordered exclusively through the
Lyreco framework agreement. Orders up to EUR 250,00 do not
require separate approval. Orders above EUR 250,00 require
approval from the cost centre manager.

3.5  Pharmacy & Medical Supplies
Urgent pharmacy purchases (medication, first-aid restocking)
are permitted up to EUR 150,00 per transaction without prior
approval, provided documentation is filed same-day.

3.6  Incontinence Materials
Incontinence materials (ABENA contract) are budgeted at
EUR 2,10 per client per day. Orders exceeding this threshold
require approval from the care coordinator.
