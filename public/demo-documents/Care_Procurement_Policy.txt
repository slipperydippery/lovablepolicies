         CARE PROCUREMENT POLICY — ZORGGROEP DE LINDEN
              Sector: Verpleging, Verzorging & Thuiszorg
                    Version 3.1 — March 2024
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.  SCOPE
This policy applies to all purchasing activities related to
direct client care across nursing homes, assisted living
facilities, and home care operations.

2.  MEDICAL SUPPLIES & PHARMACEUTICALS
2.1 Emergency ward supplies (bandages, OTC medication,
    hygiene products) may be purchased up to EUR 50,00
    per transaction at the nearest pharmacy or drugstore.

2.2 Pharmaceutical purchases for stock replenishment are
    permitted up to EUR 150,00 per run. The on-duty nurse
    is authorized to make these purchases.

3.  INCONTINENCE MATERIALS
3.1 ZorgGroep De Linden has a framework agreement with
    ABENA for incontinence products. The budgeted rate is
    EUR 2,10 per client per day.

3.2 Non-contract purchases of incontinence materials require
    written justification from the care coordinator.

4.  NUTRITION & GROCERIES
4.1 Daily grocery purchases for residential groups are
    capped at EUR 15,00 per transaction. Only approved
    supermarkets may be used.

5.  OFFICE SUPPLIES
5.1 All office supply orders must go through the Lyreco
    framework agreement. Single orders up to EUR 100,00
    may be placed without approval. Orders above EUR 100,00
    require the cost centre manager's sign-off.

6.  TEMPORARY STAFFING
6.1 Temporary staff may only be engaged through approved
    agencies (currently: AB-Zorg Uitzendbureau).
6.2 No per-transaction limit applies; however, all bookings
    must reference a valid cost centre and purchase order.
